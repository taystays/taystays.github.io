1 dinosaurs before dark

2023/7/1
“Magic Tree House: Dinosaurs Before Dark” is a children’s book written by Mary Pope Osborne. It is the first book in the popular Magic Tree House series. The story follows the adventures of two siblings , Jack and Annie, who discover a magical tree house filled with books.

In “Dinosaurs Before Dark,” Jack and Annie stumble upon the mysterious tree house in the woods near their home. They soon realize that the tree house can transport them to different places and times throughout history. As they enter the tree house, they find a book about dinosaurs.

Curious and excited, Jack and Annie wish to travel back in time to see real dinosaurs. Suddenly, the tree house starts to spin and shake, and the siblings find themselves transported to the prehistoric era, specifically to the time of the dinosaurs.

Upon their arrival , Jack and Annie encounter various types of dinosaurs, such as a Triceratops, a Stegosaurus, and even a Tyrannosaurus rex. They explore the prehistoric world, witnessing the behaviors and habitats of these magnificent creatures.

However, their adventure takes a thrilling turn when they realize they need to find a way back to the present. Along their journey, they face challenges, encounter dangerous situations, and learn important lessons about bravery and resourcefulness.

With the help of a dinosaur named a Pteranodon, Jack and Annie manage to return to the tree house and make their way back home. They excitedly share their incredible adventure with their friends and family, but the magic tree house still holds countless more adventures awaiting them.

“Dinosaurs Before Dark” introduces young readers to the concept of time travel and dinosaurs, blending education and entertainment seamlessly . It sparks children’s imaginations, allowing them to explore the wonders of the past through the eyes of Jack and Annie.
